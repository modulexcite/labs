Running Bionic Pancreas Offline

The web app has been setup to be accesible offline. The browser will save the app into its cache after first visiting the url labs-server.tidepool.io:8087. An alert will infrom you when the app is availabe to use offline.

Add to home screen on an Android device

  • Open labs-server.tidepool.io:8087 on the Chrome Web Browser
  • Click top dropdown on the top bar. Select add to homescreen
  • Navigate your applications and find the Bionic Pancreas app

Mirror App to Yota phone e-paper when online

  • Start the bionic pancreas app
  • Press and hold the Home button. Three choices will showup. Slide your finger to the left most icon
  • Flip the phone to find the homescreen mirrored on the epaper screen

The server, http://labs-server.tidepool.io, hosts tidepool labs homepage along with all its projects.
